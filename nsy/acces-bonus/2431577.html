Case n°2431577

Inspector in charge: D.I. Lestrade


Flames quickly spread to all four floors of the building in Sherbrooke Way, Worcester Park (South-West London), after fire crews were called just before 01:30 BST. The building is made up of 23 flats and based in The Hamptons estate, an American-style complex of social and private housing which was built on a former sewage treatment works.

One of the residents, Darren Nicholson, said he woke up to the sound of "crackling" and when he opened the curtains he saw the flames and got himself and the family out. He said fire alarms were going off in the community areas but not in his own flat and he believed the blaze began "on the balconies".

No witness. No suspect.

All four storeys of the block were ablaze

London Fire Brigade said it was a "challenging" blaze to tackle
