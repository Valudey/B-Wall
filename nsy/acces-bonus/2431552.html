The fire in New City Road, Plaistow, began shortly after 22:00 BST (24/07/2019) and "destroyed" the substation but there are no reports of injuries, London Fire Brigade said. People living near to New City School have been left without power. An electrical station caught fire near the primary school. It appears that the electrical substation was damaged before the fire. There was no programmed maintenance that week. The damages were caused by a human being, judging by the traces found on it.
